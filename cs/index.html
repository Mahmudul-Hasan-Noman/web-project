About Our Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Barishal University is committed to excellence in education, research, and service. Established in 2010, our department has grown to become one of the leading CSE departments in Bangladesh.

We offer comprehensive undergraduate and graduate programs that prepare students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving field of computer science and engineering.
